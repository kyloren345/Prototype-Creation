App Compatibility

With the Transrapid app you get to use special features that enable you to have the best experience with your Transrapid and make the best use of it.

Autopark

With Autopark you will get to use your Transrapid's cameras to detect a parking spot for you to park in. It helps you to find out where a parking spot is without you realising it at first.

Summon

With Summon you get to summon literally your module from the app where it will come from a Boring Parking Lot underground and will get to your house safely and efficiently.

Media Controls

With Media Controls you can change which music tracks you are listening to from your Phone or even your module VIA keyboard and mouse. It also makes it so that it will automatically detect a music pattern by remebering what songs you listen to at what time and will ask you if you want that song.

Enhanced Autopilot

With Enhanced Autopilot you get to Autosteer on city streets which will help you to get to destinations in a better manner as then the Boring Tunnels aren't being used so often to go to one Helicopter pickup area.

Solar Panel Implementation

With Solar Panels you are able to travel where ever you want without worrying about stopping to charge as you can charge as you move. It can help you to travel long distances in only a short amount of time VIA the fact that you can travel really fast with your Helicopter carrier.

Super Network Features

With Super Network Features you can make sure that you get to wherever you need to be in the shortest amount of time VIA the Boring Super tunnels designed only for the Transrapid L0 Alpha model.
