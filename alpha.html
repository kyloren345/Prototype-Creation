What are the features of our Prototype

Our Prototype features multiple safety features that are subjective to not only our design but also inspiration from; Tesla, Airbus x Audi, Formula 1, and also Mercedes, and MagLev we implemented Autopilot and Enhanced Autopilot, helicopter carriages, finding the nearest parking place for the helicopter and the carriages are taken by a train with the same implementations from MagLev. Also there is a huge luggage compartment at the front of the bullet train like nose.

What it's purpose is

The purpose of our Prototype is to take you anywhere quickly and also to do it safely. How we do this you ask? Well we implemented AI that you programme yourself, now don't worry you don't need any computing knowledge all you have to do is tell the AI by typing on the keyboard to find the nearest helicopter. The process is simple all you do is get into your own personal carriage that is like a train capsule but YOU are the one who controls it, just get in and type your nearest destination to get picked up by a helicopter module and wait for the arrival. Then your train section will get separated from the carriage section and you will be able to lift off and head to your destination same thing just type in where you want to go and your module will take you there. As for your train carriage that took you there well we have incoporated similar technology to Tesla's Autopilot and it will find the nearest parking area like the Mercedes future car will do. There is also the feature of the rail connecting to your house to make availability to summon, and there will be more features.
